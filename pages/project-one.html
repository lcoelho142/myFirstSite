JOY in Connection

The goal for this project was to design an engaging, competitive -yet silly- card game that could easily be integrated into your next family game night.

The visuals consist of simplistic cat-themed illustrations in a variety of vibrant colors, setting a youthful and energetic tone.

OPTIMA

PRIMARY FONT

This humanist sans-serif typeface with flared terminals at the end of strokes give it a uniquely sophisticated appearance. The wide frame of characters and high stroke contrast makes it an exceptionally versatile font for the project.

PROXIMA NOVA

SECONDARY FONT

This geometric sans-serif typeface with modern proportions brings clean, modern lines and readability to the smaller body text of this project.
