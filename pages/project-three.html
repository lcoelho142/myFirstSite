FAITH in Design

The goal was to create a logo that resonates with the church's mission: “To help each person discover their new life 'In Christ', by walking out their faith, igniting their passion, and unleashing their purpose!”

I decided on using a butterfly as the dominant iconography because it's universally known as a symbol for new beginnings, hope, and rebirth. While experimenting in the design software, I realized a christianity cross would fit beautifully as the body of the butterfly.

OPTIMA

PRIMARY FONT

This humanist sans-serif typeface with flared terminals at the end of strokes give it a uniquely sophisticated appearance. The wide frame of characters and high stroke contrast makes it an exceptionally versatile font for the project.
